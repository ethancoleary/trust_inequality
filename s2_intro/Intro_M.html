Stage 2 Intro

Pay attention to the following instructions as you will soon need to pass a comprehension check.

For stage 2, you have been grouped with 299 other participants to form a society. You will be shortly matched with someone from your society.

Each participant in your society will be assigned to a group (A or B). This assignment will be made according to how their individual score in stage 1 compared to the scores of a group of participants in a previous study. A participant in your society will be assigned to group A if their score is at least as high as the average score in the previous study. Alternatively, they will be assigned to group B is their score is lower than the average score in the previous study.

In this previous study, participants had 2 minutes to decrypt as many codes as they could. They faced the same sequence of codes and decryption keys as all participants in the current study.

The group assignment determines each participant's initial bonus for stage 2. Those assigned to group A will receive E£80 and those assigned to group B will receive E£40.

Click on 'next' to find out what group you have been assigned to and who you have been matched with.
