Stage 2 Information

Pay attention to the following instructions as you will soon need to pass a comprehension check.

You were randomly assigned to group {{ g }}.

For the rest of stage 2, you have been matched with a participant from your society who was randomly assigned to group {{ m }}.

In other words, for stage 2, you have been randomly assigned an initial bonus of E£{{ endowment }} and your match has been randomly assigned an initial bonus of E£{{ m_endowment }}.

During stage 2, you will interact with your match in two games. You will each make your decisions simultaneously and you will not learn the outcome of each other's decisions until the end of the study. The games will be played in a random order.

In each game, there are two roles (first mover and second mover) and therefore two situations. Each situation corresponds to a possible allocation of roles between you and your match. You will make decisions for each situation i.e. for each possible allocation of roles.

This means that during stage 2, you will make decisions in 4 situations (2 decisions for each of 2 games).

At the end of this stage, one of the 4 situations will be chosen at random. Your bonus payment will then be equal to your final bonus amount once your randomly selected situation is paid out according to your and your match's decisions.

Therefore, it is in your best interest to treat every decision as if you will be paid for it.
