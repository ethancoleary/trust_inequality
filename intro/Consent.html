Introduction

Welcome to this study. During your participation, you will make a number of decisions that will affect your bonus payments. The study will last approximately 10-12 minutes and you will receive a participation fee of £1.20.

It is always in your best interest to answer all questions in this study honestly.

To monitor attention, this study tracks whether you switch tabs/windows and how long you spend on each page. We ask that you do not perform any other tasks while completing this experiment to give us your full attention.

To complete the study, you will have to pass some comprehension questions.

Consent

All data collected in this experiment is anonymised. Only anonymous data may be shared in open science repositories. At any point in the experiment, you may opt out and return your submission. Incomplete responses will be discarded before analysis.

All information in this experiment referring to other participants is true and relates to real human participants. The decisions which you make in this experiment impact your remuneration and the remuneration of other individuals.

There are no known risks to this experiment and you are not required to provide any personally identifiable information.

This study has received ethical approval from the Norwegian School of Economics' Institutional Review Board. Should you have any further questions regarding this study, please contact ethan.oleary@nhh.no.

Should you agree to these terms, please indicate your consent below. If you disagree, you may withdraw now by closing your browser and return your submission.
